About us

Edumat is a platform where students from anywhere can get free courses that are lead by our ambitious teachers. Our goal is to be able to help students learn and acquire new skills at home. All you need is a device and internet connection and also you need a deep desire for learning.

All of our courses are more than 2 hours long. So if you have any questions, or not understanding a topic, or any type of problems, you can always connect with our teacher at any time. They will try their best to teach you and help you understand.
